7 Main Theorem

Theorem 35 Approximate Gumbel Convergence
#

Assume the properties in Definitions 27, 28, and 29. Let \(\alpha {\gt} 2/3\) and \(N_n = \lfloor n^\alpha \rfloor \). For a sequence of Gumbel grids \(Y^{(n)}\) and any \(r \in \mathbb {R}\):

\[ \mathbb {P}\left(\frac{T_{\text{Approx}}^{N_n}(n) - C_g \cdot n}{\sigma _g \cdot n^{1/3}} \leq r\right) \to F_{\text{GUE}}(r) \]

as \(n \to \infty \).

Proof ▶

By Theorems 23 and 24:

\[ 0 \leq T_{\text{Approx}}^{N_n}(n) - T_{\text{Gumbel}}(n) \leq \frac{1}{N_n} L_{\text{Exp}}(n) \]

Dividing by \(\sigma _g n^{1/3}\):

\[ \left|\frac{T_{\text{Approx}}^{N_n}(n) - T_{\text{Gumbel}}(n)}{\sigma _g n^{1/3}}\right| \leq \frac{L_{\text{Exp}}(n)}{N_n \sigma _g n^{1/3}} \]

By Definition 29, \(L_{\text{Exp}}(n)/n \xrightarrow {\mathbb {P}} D_L\). By Lemma 34, \(n/(N_n n^{1/3}) \to 0\). Therefore by Lemma 33:

\[ \frac{T_{\text{Approx}}^{N_n}(n) - T_{\text{Gumbel}}(n)}{\sigma _g n^{1/3}} \xrightarrow {\mathbb {P}} 0 \]

By Definition 27:

\[ \frac{T_{\text{Gumbel}}(n) - C_g n}{\sigma _g n^{1/3}} \xrightarrow {d} F_{\text{GUE}} \]

Applying Slutsky’s Theorem (Theorem 32) completes the proof.
