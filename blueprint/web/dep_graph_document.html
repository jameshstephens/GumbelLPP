Definition 10Gumbel Grid

A random field \(Y : \text{Edge} \to \Omega \to \mathbb {R}\) is a Gumbel grid if:

  1. The random variables \(\{ Y_e\} _{e \in \text{Edge}}\) are independent, and

  2. For each edge \(e\) and \(x \in \mathbb {R}\), \(\mathbb {P}(Y_e \leq x) = F_{\text{Gumbel}}(x)\).
